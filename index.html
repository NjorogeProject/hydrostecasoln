About Us

Since its inception in 2013 and its incorporation in 2017, Hydrosteca Solutions has become the leading dealer in the installation and setup of Pumps, boreholes, Swimming pools. Its recognition by the world’s greatest pump dealer, Dayliff & Shirtliff, alongside being crowned the 2017 best installer of Swimming pool equipment, Hydrosteca Solutions continues to compete unparalleled in the industry.

OUR VISION

To offer nothing but excellence in providing quality, effective, and efficient products and services to our clients.

OUR MISSION

Ensuring client satisfaction through client involvement, support, and feedback acknowledgement.

Accredited dealers of Davis & Shirtliff products.
